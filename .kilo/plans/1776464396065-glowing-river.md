3. **Produce Structured Output**: Deliver findings in a clear, actionable format that can guide future development and decision-making.

### Step 4: Synthesis and Recommendations

- Highlight highest-probability failure modes and mitigation priorities.
- Provide actionable recommendations for proceeding to implementation.
